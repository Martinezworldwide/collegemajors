Mechanical Engineering Curriculum

Explore the complete curriculum for Mechanical Engineering, including recommended open-source textbooks.

Freshman Year

  1. ENES100 - Introduction to Engineering Design - "Engineering Design: A Project-Based Introduction" by Clive L. Dym, Patrick Little, and Elizabeth Orwin
  2. MATH140 - Calculus I - OpenStax Calculus Vol. 1
  3. MATH141 - Calculus II - OpenStax Calculus Vol. 2
  4. CHEM135 - Chemistry for Engineers - OpenStax Chemistry: Atoms First
  5. CHEM136 - Chemistry Laboratory for Engineers - OpenStax Chemistry: Atoms First (Lab Concepts Included)
  6. PHYS161 - General Physics: Mechanics and Particle Dynamics - OpenStax University Physics Vol. 1
  7. ENGL101 - Academic Writing - Writing for Success
  8. General Education Elective (Humanities/History) - World History: Cultures, States, and Societies to 1500

Sophomore Year

  1. PHYS260 - General Physics: Electromagnetism, Oscillations, and Waves - OpenStax University Physics Vol. 2
  2. PHYS261 - General Physics Laboratory - "Physics Laboratory Experiments" by Jerry D. Wilson, Cecilia A. Hernández, and Charles A. Lutz
  3. MATH241 - Calculus III - OpenStax Calculus Vol. 3
  4. MATH246 - Differential Equations - Paul Dawkins (Internet Archive), Paul Dawkins (Toomey.org)
  5. ENES102 - Mechanics I (Statics) - "Engineering Mechanics: Statics" by J.L. Meriam and L.G. Kraige
  6. ENES220 - Mechanics II (Dynamics) - "Engineering Mechanics: Dynamics" by J.L. Meriam and L.G. Kraige
  7. ENES221 - Dynamics and Vibration - "Mechanical Vibrations" by Singiresu S. Rao
  8. ENES232 - Thermodynamics - "Thermodynamics: An Engineering Approach" by Yunus A. Çengel and Michael A. Boles
  9. General Education Elective (Social Sciences) - OpenStax Introduction to Sociology 3e

Junior Year

  1. ENME272 - Introduction to Computer-Aided Design - "Engineering Graphics Essentials with AutoCAD" by Kirstie Plantenberg
  2. ENME331 - Fluid Mechanics - "Fluid Mechanics" by Frank M. White
  3. ENME332 - Transfer Processes (Heat and Mass Transfer) - "Heat and Mass Transfer: Fundamentals and Applications" by Yunus A. Çengel and Afshin J. Ghajar
  4. ENME350 - Electronics and Instrumentation I - "Introduction to Mechatronics and Measurement Systems" by David G. Alciatore and Michael B. Histand
  5. ENME351 - Electronics and Instrumentation Laboratory - "LabVIEW for Engineers" by Ronald W. Larsen
  6. ENME361 - Vibrations, Controls, and Optimization I - "Mechanical Vibrations" by Singiresu S. Rao
  7. ENME371 - Product Engineering and Manufacturing - "Manufacturing Processes for Engineering Materials" by Serope Kalpakjian and Steven R. Schmid
  8. ENME462 - Vibrations, Controls, and Optimization II - "Theory of Vibration with Applications" by William T. Thomson and Marie Dillon Dahleh
  9. ENME382 - Mechanics of Materials - "Mechanics of Materials" by Ferdinand P. Beer, E. Russell Johnston Jr., and John T. DeWolf
  10. ENME383 - Mechanics of Materials Laboratory - "Mechanics of Materials Laboratory Manual" by R.C. Hibbeler

Senior Year

  1. ENME472 - Integrated Product and Process Development - "Product Design and Development" by Karl T. Ulrich and Steven D. Eppinger
  2. ENME481 - Capstone Design Project I - "Engineering Design: A Systematic Approach" by Gerhard Pahl, Wolfgang Beitz, and Jörg Feldhusen
  3. ENME482 - Capstone Design Project II - "The Mechanical Design Process" by David G. Ullman
  4. ENME489X - Special Topics in Mechanical Engineering - "Mechanical Engineering Design" by Shigley, Mischke, Budynas, and Nisbett
  5. Technical Elective 1 (Specialization Course) - "Fundamentals of Engineering Thermodynamics" by Michael J. Moran and Howard N. Shapiro
  6. Technical Elective 2 (Specialization Course) - "Introduction to Finite Element Analysis Using MATLAB and Abaqus" by Amar Khennane
  7. Technical Elective 3 (Specialization Course) - "Control Systems Engineering" by Norman S. Nise
  8. General Education Elective (I-Series) - Course-specific materials provided by the instructor
  9. General Education Elective (Diversity) - Course-specific materials, articles, or online resources

Electives

Design and Manufacturing

  4. ENME489A - Additive Manufacturing (3D Printing) - "Additive Manufacturing Technologies: 3D Printing, Rapid Prototyping, and Direct Digital Manufacturing" by Ian Gibson, David W. Rosen, and Brent Stucker

Robotics and Mechatronics

  1. ENME444 - Mechatronics and Embedded Systems - "Introduction to Mechatronics and Measurement Systems" by David G. Alciatore and Michael B. Histand

Aerospace and Automotive

  4. ENME489M - Medical Robotics - "Medical Instrumentation: Application and Design" by John G. Webster
